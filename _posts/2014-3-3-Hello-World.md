title: The Seq2Seq Architecture with Sequential Models
---

This blogpost, as the title conveys, explains the Seq2Seq architecture consisting of an encoder side and a decoder side when both sides use an RNN (Recurrent Neural Network) as the model architecture. We start from understanding the mechanics of how the Seq2Seq architecture works, and then move on to understanding the concept of "Attention" - a mechanism that, when applied to the Seq2Seq architecture, derives better performance out of it.
